## Tweet 2 — Agents vs Chatbots

```
Chatbots operate on a request-response loop.
One input. One output. No persistence.

Agents operate on a goal-execution loop.
They decompose objectives, select tools, act, observe outcomes, and iterate.

The distinction is architectural, not cosmetic.
```
